# Email Setup Guide - Web3Forms

## Quick Setup (2 minutes)

1. **Get Your Access Key:**
   - Go to https://web3forms.com
   - Enter your email: **pavandeeptech@gmail.com**
   - Click "Get Access Key"
   - Copy the access key they send to your email

2. **Add Key to Your Code:**
   - Open `components/Contact.tsx`
   - Find line with: `access_key: 'YOUR_WEB3FORMS_ACCESS_KEY'`
   - Replace `YOUR_WEB3FORMS_ACCESS_KEY` with your actual key

3. **Done!** 
   - Messages will now be sent to pavandeeptech@gmail.com
   - No account needed, completely free
   - Works instantly

## Example:
```typescript
access_key: 'a1b2c3d4-e5f6-7890-abcd-ef1234567890', // Your actual key
```
