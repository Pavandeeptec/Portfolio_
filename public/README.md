# Public Assets Folder

## Add Your Profile Picture

1. **Prepare your photo:**
   - Use a professional headshot or portrait
   - Square aspect ratio works best (1:1)
   - Recommended size: 800x800px or larger
   - Format: JPG or PNG

2. **Add to this folder:**
   - Save your photo as `profile.jpg` in this `public` folder
   - Path should be: `public/profile.jpg`

3. **Alternative names:**
   - If you use a different name, update `components/About.tsx`
   - Change `src="/profile.jpg"` to your filename

## Tips for Best Results:
- ✅ Good lighting
- ✅ Clean background (or use a background remover tool)
- ✅ Professional attire
- ✅ Friendly, confident expression
- ✅ High resolution (at least 800x800px)

## Free Tools to Edit Your Photo:
- **Remove background:** remove.bg
- **Enhance quality:** photopea.com
- **Resize:** squoosh.app
